Privacy Policy

Improv Practice · Last updated: June 28, 2026

The short version: Improv Practice does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts and no sign-in. The app works fully offline, and the little data it saves (your favorites, recently viewed games, and practice history) stays only on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Improv Practice mobile application (the “App”) handles information. It applies to your use of the App on iOS. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App has no user accounts, no login, and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information. The App does not contain analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services, and includes no third-party software development kits that collect data.

Information stored on your device

To make the App useful, it saves a small amount of information locally on your device only:

  • Games and drills you mark as favorites
  • The games you have recently viewed
  • Your practice history (a count of completed sessions and your daily-streak dates)

This information is stored using your device’s standard local app storage. It never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or to anyone else, and is not accessible to us. It is removed automatically if you delete the App.

Network use

The App is designed to work entirely offline and does not make network requests. It does not send any information to us or to third parties, and it does not require an internet connection to function.

Device features the App uses

The App uses the following on-device capabilities purely to function. None of these transmit any data:

  • Vibration / haptics — for tactile feedback when you tap controls.
  • Text-to-speech — the device’s built-in speech synthesizer reads short prompts aloud (for example, the “New Choice” caller). This is processed locally by the operating system; no text or audio is sent off your device.

The App does not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, calendar, or health data.

Children’s privacy

Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it collects no information from children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and no such data is gathered by the App from any user.

Data retention and deletion

Because all saved information lives only on your device, you are in full control of it. Deleting the App removes all of its locally stored data. You can also clear the App’s data through your device’s settings.

Security

Your information stays on your device within the protections provided by iOS’s app sandboxing. Because nothing is transmitted or stored on external servers, there is no remote database that could be breached.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
